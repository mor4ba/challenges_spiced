    Hi that's me!

    Born in the excellent year of 1993, i grew up in the northwest of germany, quite on the countryside. The absence of nearly every possible thing young people could be interested in at that age, we just packed up in groups of people trying to have a good time - more often than i'd like it to be, this meant to get baked and watch the clouds, play frisbee or cards.

    It took me close to 20 years to move to a bigger city to finally fail at some university degree while discovering the benefits of meeting and spending time with beautiful people around me, my passion to work my ass off in the kitchen developed to a frenzy, close to stopping me from graduating in my second university degree. Finally, finishing the just mentionend degree brought me to berlin and i never spend a second thinking about going back.
